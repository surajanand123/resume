About Dayananda Sagar University

Dayananda Sagar University, established in 2014, is a premier university in Bangalore, India, known for its commitment to excellence in education and research. With state-of-the-art facilities, a vibrant campus life, and a strong emphasis on innovation and entrepreneurship, DSU prepares students to meet global challenges.

Programs Offered

DSU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in Engineering, Arts and Humanities, Commerce and Management, and Health Sciences.

  • Engineering: Computer Science, Mechanical, Electronics & Communication, Civil
  • Business & Management: MBA, BBA
  • Health Sciences: B.Pharm, M.Pharm, Physiotherapy
  • Arts & Humanities: BA in Journalism, English, Psychology

Admissions

DSU welcomes students from all over the world. Our admission process is designed to select students who have the potential to become global leaders. For detailed information on how to apply, scholarship opportunities, and deadlines, please visit our Admissions page or contact our Admissions Office.
